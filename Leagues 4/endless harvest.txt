Lumbridge - Lap 1
-----------------
(Note: if worried about competition, rush the Copper and Tin first)
Pickpocket a man
Do it again 28 times and open all coin pouches at once
Grab items from Sage
Get Ironman armour
Visit Death
Dance in Lumbridge Graveyard
Mine Copper and Tin until 15 Mining
Catch Shrimp until 5 Fishing (get a few more to make sure you can level Cooking)
Misthalin Mystery (take 2x bucket and knife during quest, we will need them)

Karamja - Lap 1
---------------
Teleport to Brimhaven
Pick Pineapple
Ride cart to Shilo Village
Sell unneeded items to the General Store
Buy Torch from General Store and light it
Bank
Buy Fishing Rod, Fly Fishing Rod and bait (leave a bit of extra cash)
Cast Home Teleport from the Spellbook (not the area teleport)

Lumbridge - Lap 2
-----------------
Start Rune Mysteries and locate with the Air Talisman
Start Cook's Assistant
Bank (store everything except coins, buckets, pot, axe, shears)
Visit General Store, make sure you have shears, pot, 2 buckets
Kill Goblin
Bury Bones
Milk a cow TWICE (one for Gertrude)
Pick up an egg (chicken coop near the windmill or farm near cows)
Start Sheep Shearer
Shear 20 Sheep
Cry in Wheat Field
Make Flour
Chop the tree in the potato field near the windmill
Enter Draynor Manor
Get a chair to follow you
Teleport back to Lumbridge
Make 20 Wool
Finish Cook's Assistant
Cook something on the Lumbridge Range
Rake the Tree Patch
Finish Sheep Shearer

Draynor - Lap 1
---------------
Insult Aggie
Complete Draynor Rooftop course 10 times and until you get a Mark of Grace
Start Vampyre Slayer
Get a task from the Wise Old Man
Make Wise Old Man angry (ask about his hat and tell him he robbed the bank, may need to see the robbery recording first)
Bank
Grab Forestry set
Chop trees until 15 WC
Chop Oak
Fletch Arrow shafts
Burn logs
Burn Oak logs
Cook Shrimp
Burn something
Rod fish until 15, then catch an anchovy, then fish until 20 (note anchovies only heal 1)
Use a Herring on a tree (!)
Cook enough food to do Stronghold of Security (keep a Sardine for Gertrude's Cat)
(Don't do more WC yet - wait until you can farm for a Rune Axe)
Do Rune Mysteries step in Wizard's Tower
Use Wizard's Tower Fairy ring to enter Zanaris, then go to Edgeville (DKR)

Edgeville
---------
Smelt Bronze
Get a Slayer Task from Vannaka
Buy Slayer Gem and use it to check task
Stronghold of Security, equip the boots

Varrock
-------
Start Gertrude's Cat (pick up Doogle Leaves)
Smith Bronze (Full Helm and Plateskirt are tasks, get some Nails for Daddy's Home)
Romeo and Juliet
Sell unneeded Bronze gear
Buy and equip an Elemental Staff (Air or Fire probably best)
Get the Stake for Vampyre Slayer
Pet a stray dog
Steal some Tea (don't drink it or drop it, needed for Elsie)
Do Rune Mysteries Aubury step
Natural History Quiz
Have Elsie tell you a story
Slash a web in the sewers
Go to Fossil Island
Pet the dog on Fossil Island
Make the Fossil Island bank if possible (21 Cons, don't think it's quite possible yet)
Grab some planks on the floor to save some cash
Daddy's Home (do Gertrude's Cat while there too)
Mine a few Iron (don't need many yet, wait for Steel Pick)
Complete Gertrude's Cat (do not drop the stew reward, bank it)

Zanaris
-------
Fairy Ring to Zanaris
Catch a Baby Impling in Puro Puro
Fairy Ring CKR to Karamja (CKR)
Catch a Karambwanji (be careful of the Hobgoblin)
Fairy Ring to Wizard's Tower (DIS)

Draynor - Lap 2
---------------
Finish Rune Mysteries
Teleport to the Essence Mine
Give the Draynor Guard the Stew from Gertrude's Cat
Get Ned to make 3 Rope buy talking to him (don't use the shop interface)

Karamja - Lap 2
---------------
Buy a Lobster Pot from the Traders in Brimhaven
Enter POH
Build a Parlour in POH
Enter the Brimhaven Dungeon (need an Axe)
Fill a crate with bananas
Go to Shilo Village and catch 50 Salmon

Lumbridge - Lap 3
-----------------
Get a Steel Axe and cut a tree with it
Smelt an Iron Bar
Use the free runes from the Magic Tutor to cast Air Strike a few times
Find a farmer to pickpocket until 15 Thieving
Pickpocket HAM members for an Iron Pickaxe
Ideally get 13 Magic, buy some Fire and Mind Runes and try to kill a Tree Spirit for a Rune Axe

Freeform from here
------------------
Should have completed well over 70 tasks at this point
Probably have another relic and/or another region.
Work through the skilling tasks e.g. 100 lobs, 100 willows, 50 Iron
Start training combats
At some point after getting 5 Def buy a Spiny Helm and equip it
